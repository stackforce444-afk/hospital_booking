Symptoms

  • Chronic cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Wheezing
  • Chest tightness
  • Frequent respiratory infections

Preventions

  • Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get vaccinated for flu and pneumonia
  • Use a mask in polluted environments
  • Maintain good indoor air quality
