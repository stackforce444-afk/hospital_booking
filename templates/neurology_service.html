Understanding Neurological Health

Warning Signs

  • Persistent headaches
  • Dizziness or balance problems
  • Memory problems
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Seizures
  • Vision problems

Risk Factors

  • Family history of neurological disorders
  • High blood pressure
  • Diabetes
  • Smoking
  • Age
  • Previous head trauma

Prevention Tips

  • Regular exercise
  • Healthy diet
  • Mental stimulation
  • Adequate sleep
  • Stress management
  • Wearing protective gear during activities
  • Regular check-ups
