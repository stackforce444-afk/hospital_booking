Kidney Disease Awareness

⚠️ Symptoms

  • 🔴 Swelling in ankles, feet, or hands
  • 💤 Fatigue or weakness
  • 🌙 Frequent urination, especially at night
  • 🔄 Changes in urine color or smell
  • 🥴 Nausea and loss of appetite
  • 😕 Difficulty concentrating
  • 😣 Persistent itching

🛡️ Protection Tips

  • 🚰 Stay hydrated – drink 2–3 liters of water daily
  • 🥗 Eat a kidney-friendly diet (low salt, balanced protein)
  • 🏃‍♀️ Exercise regularly to maintain healthy blood pressure
  • 💊 Avoid overuse of painkillers and self-medication
  • 🩺 Get regular kidney function tests
  • 🚭 Quit smoking & limit alcohol intake
  • 🧂 Reduce processed food and sodium intake
