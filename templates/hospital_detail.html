About Care Hospital

Care Hospital is a multi-specialty healthcare facility committed to providing exceptional medical care with compassion and expertise. Established in 2005, our hospital has grown to become one of the leading healthcare providers in the region.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, combined with our team of experienced medical professionals, ensure that patients receive the highest standard of care. We pride ourselves on our patient-centered approach, focusing on personalized treatment plans and comprehensive care.

Location & Directions

How to Reach

By Car: Located on Health Avenue, easily accessible from the main highway. Free parking available for patients and visitors.

By Public Transport: Bus routes 101, 203, and 305 stop directly in front of the hospital. The nearest metro station is Medical Center (1 km away).

From Airport: 15 km from International Airport. Taxi services available at the airport.
