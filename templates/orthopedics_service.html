Understanding Bone & Joint Health

Warning Signs

  • Persistent joint pain
  • Joint swelling or stiffness
  • Limited range of motion
  • Unusual sounds when moving joints
  • Recurring back pain
  • Muscle weakness

Risk Factors

  • Age
  • Previous injuries
  • Overuse or repetitive stress
  • Obesity
  • Family history of joint disorders
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Poor posture

Prevention Tips

  • Regular exercise
  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Proper lifting techniques
  • Ergonomic workspace
  • Calcium and vitamin D intake
  • Avoid overtraining
  • Proper warm-up before activities
  • Use protective gear during sports
