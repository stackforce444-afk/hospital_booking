Child Health & Development

Important Milestones

  • First smile (2 months)
  • Rolling over (4 months)
  • Sitting without support (6 months)
  • First words (12 months)
  • Walking (12-15 months)
  • Speaking in sentences (24 months)

Common Concerns

  • Fever
  • Ear infections
  • Respiratory infections
  • Digestive issues
  • Skin rashes
  • Sleep problems
  • Behavioral challenges

Healthy Habits

  • Regular check-ups
  • Complete immunizations
  • Balanced nutrition
  • Adequate sleep
  • Physical activity
  • Limited screen time
  • Good hygiene practices
  • Positive parenting
